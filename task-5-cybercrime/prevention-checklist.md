### **'Stay Safe Online' checklist**






* ###### Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on email, social media and banking or any accounts carrying personal data.
* ###### Never scan a code and use your UPI transcation pin to recieve money.
* ###### Use your banking apps to set transaction limits to minize loss in case your pin or password is compromised.
* ###### Limit your profile visibility to freinds only, scammers ofter use public photos to create fake AI clones or blackmail students.
* ###### Do not use same password for each account. Use a password manager to generate and store unique passwords.
* ###### Use secure Wi-Fi networks; avoid logging into important accounts on public Wi-Fi. 
* ###### Log out from your accounts on public devices after each session.
* ###### Think before sharing personal information such as travel plans, photos, passwords etc on Social media platforms.
* ###### Always verify payment requests and never make any transaction in a state of hurry.
* ###### Do not use pirated softwares or media as it puts your device at risk.
* ###### Avoid scanning unknown OR codes or clicking on random links.


* #### IF you ever find yourself a victim of Cybercrime report immediately on the National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in or Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 especially for financial frauds
